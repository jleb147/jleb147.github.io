GOLGOTHAN

Bringing Brutal Theatric Death Metal to Louisiana since 2013.

Want More? Don't Care?

Why Golgothan?

Singer Jai Benoit asked himself in 2013 an important question. What image best exemplifies the aggressive, off-putting, and brutal music he conceptualized in his head? Simple. The demon made out of pure human excrement called the Golgothan. It made an appearance in the 1999 Kevin Smith movie Dogma. It's said to be formed from the bowels of those who crucified Christ. It stuck. Metaphorically speaking.

Members

Vocals - Jai // Guitar - Jesse // Guitar - John // Bass - Jerik // Drums - Eddie

What is Theatric Death Metal?

Imagine you are watching a terrible horror schlock movie from the 80s. Now, add highly distorted guitars, gutteral/inhuman vocals, relentless drums, and costumed freaks performing said elements.
